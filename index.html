Did You Know?

Daily tips to keep your health knowledge sharp

💧
Hydration Tip

Drinking adequate water helps regulate body temperature and keep joints lubricated. Aim for 8 glasses a day.

🥗
Nutrition Tip

A balanced diet rich in fruits and vegetables can lower the risk of chronic diseases and improve energy levels.

🏃
Exercise Tip

Regular physical activity improves cardiovascular health and releases mood-boosting endorphins. 30 mins a day makes a big difference!

😴
Sleep Tip

Getting 7–9 hours of quality sleep supports cognitive function and body recovery after workouts.

🥩
Protein Tip

Consume various protein sources like lean meats and nuts for muscle repair and growth.
